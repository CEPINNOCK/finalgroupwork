Here is some Useful Information to help you

  • Use FreshStart: FreshStart is a great resource for finding information about the local city and connecting with other students.
  • Attend Orientation: Attend the university orientation to learn about the resources available to you, such as student clubs, academic advising, and campus events.
  • Check the University Website: The university website is a great place to find information about course schedules, campus maps, and student services.
  • Ask Your Professors: Professors are an excellent resource for academic support and advice. Don't be afraid to ask them questions about the coursework or the university.
  • Talk to Upperclassmen: Upperclassmen can provide valuable insights into the university, including the best study spots, local restaurants, and extracurricular activitie
